What is IXThemes?

 

IXThemes is an implementation of innovative methods in the development of qualitative universality themes and templates for content management systems based on the XOOPS Web application platform.

  

What is XOOPS?

 

XOOPS is an extensible, OO (Object Oriented), easy to use dynamic web content management system written in PHP. XOOPS is the ideal tool for developing small to large dynamic community websites, intra company portals, corporate portals.
